About Student Guide

An AI-powered resume analyzer helping students bridge the gap between their skills and job market demands

Our Mission

Student Guide is designed to empower students and fresh graduates by providing them with personalized, AI-driven insights into their resumes. We believe that every student deserves a fair chance to understand how their qualifications align with industry demands.

Our platform analyzes your resume against multiple job roles, identifying skill gaps, highlighting strengths, and providing actionable recommendations to make you more competitive in the job market.
